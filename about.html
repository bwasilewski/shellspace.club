What is Shellspace.club?

This is a place where technology enthusiasts of all sorts can come together to communicate, share, learn and grow as people. We love art, movies, code and discourse.

Shellspace.club has no central ideals or beliefs other than the golden rule - to treat others as you wish to be treated. All communication and interaction on this server should exist in accordance with this rule.

Together we hope to make the world a little more tolerable.
